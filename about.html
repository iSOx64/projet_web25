The Tournament

The Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) is the main international men's association football competition in Africa. It is sanctioned by the Confederation of African Football (CAF) and Morocco is proud to host the 2025 edition.

This prestigious tournament will feature 24 teams competing across 6 modern venues in Morocco, showcasing the best of African football talent and Morocco's world-class sporting infrastructure.

Key Facts

  • 24 participating teams
  • 52 matches
  • 6 host cities
  • December 2024

Host Country: Morocco

Morocco, with its rich football history and modern infrastructure, is the perfect host for AFCON 2025. The country's passion for football, combined with its world-class facilities and warm hospitality, promises to make this tournament truly memorable.
