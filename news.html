Morocco's Stadiums Ready for AFCON 2025

May 15, 2025

The Royal Moroccan Football Federation has announced that all six stadiums for AFCON 2025 are ahead of schedule in their preparation. The tournament, which will run from December 2025 to January 2026, will feature matches in Casablanca, Rabat, Marrakech, Agadir, Fez, and Tangier.

The Stade Mohammed V in Casablanca and Stade Moulay Abdallah in Rabat have undergone extensive renovations to meet CAF's standards, while the newly constructed Grand Stade de Casablanca is set to be the crown jewel venue for the final match with a capacity of 93,000 spectators.
