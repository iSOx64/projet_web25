Host Stadiums

Explore the 8 world-class stadiums set to host Africa's finest football moments

World-Class Venues

Morocco boasts some of Africa's most impressive football stadiums, with state-of-the-art facilities that will provide the perfect stage for the continent's most prestigious football tournament. The AFCON 2025 will be held across six cities, featuring stadiums that combine architectural beauty with modern functionality.

All stadiums have undergone extensive renovations to meet international standards, ensuring an unforgettable experience for both players and fans. From the iconic Stade Mohammed V in Casablanca to the majestic Grand Stade de Marrakech, these venues reflect Morocco's deep passion for football.

Frequently Asked Questions

A total of 8 stadiums across 6 cities will host matches during AFCON 2025 in Morocco. These venues have been carefully selected to provide excellent facilities for players and fans alike.

The final match of AFCON 2025 will be held at the iconic Stade Mohammed V in Casablanca, which has a capacity of 67,000 spectators. This historic venue has undergone extensive renovations to prepare for the tournament.

Most of the stadium renovations are either completed or in their final stages. Three stadiums (Stade Mohammed V, Stade Adrar, and Grand Stade de Marrakech) are fully completed, while the remaining venues are between 85-95% complete with all renovations scheduled to be finished well before the tournament begins.

All stadiums for AFCON 2025 feature modern amenities including high-quality hybrid grass pitches, enhanced seating, improved media facilities, energy-efficient lighting systems, and state-of-the-art security measures. Many venues also incorporate elements of traditional Moroccan architecture in their design.

Morocco has invested in transportation infrastructure to ensure easy access to all venues. Public transportation options include buses, trams, and trains connecting major cities. Special shuttle services will be available on match days from key locations. Detailed transportation guides will be published closer to the tournament.
