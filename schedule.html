Tournament Format

The Africa Cup of Nations Morocco 2025 will feature 24 teams divided into six groups of four. The top two teams from each group, along with the four best third-placed teams, will advance to the round of 16. From there, the tournament will proceed with knockout matches until a champion is crowned.

The tournament will kick off on December 1, 2025, with the opening match in Casablanca, and will conclude with the final on January 4, 2026.

Key Dates

  • Opening Match: December 1, 2025
  • Group Stage: Dec 1-12, 2025
  • Round of 16: Dec 14-17, 2025
  • Quarter-finals: Dec 19-20, 2025
  • Semi-finals: Dec 23-24, 2025
  • Final: January 4, 2026
